STEP 4 Selection Phase

Define the New Reality

Choosing what you actually want, with precision and intention

The Teaching

This is the step where you stop circling around what you want and actually name it. Your soul has been sending you signals. It has been showing you glimpses of a life that feels more true, more alive, more like you. Step 4 is where you give that life a clear shape so the universe knows exactly where to meet you.

You are going to answer the question: what do I want to be living? What has my soul been asking for? Start with the phrase, "The timeline I am choosing to enter and give my focus and energy to includes the following details" and let yourself write freely from there. This is not a vision board exercise. This is a declaration.

Happiness and freedom are beautiful intentions, but they can take a thousand different forms. Your definition of this new timeline needs to be specific enough that you would recognize it when you stepped into it. That specificity is not about control. It is about clarity. The more clearly you can see it, the more directly you can move toward it.

Anchor Point

Every timeline needs one defining detail that anchors it. This is the thing that makes this version of your life distinctly different from the one you are currently living. It could be purpose-aligned work, a relationship, a location, or a complete identity shift.

Examples of anchor points include: moving into soul-aligned entrepreneurship, finding your life partner, relocating to a new country, or fully stepping into a new version of yourself. Pick the one that most represents the core of this timeline for you.

Before You Write

Take three slow breaths. Allow yourself to open your mind and truly feel into what would light you up. What would make you excited to wake up every morning? What does your soul actually want? Let the answers rise without judgment, then move into the reflection below.
