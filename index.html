STEP 1 SELECTION PHASE
Observe Your Current Reality
See where you are before you choose where you are going.
The Teaching

Before you can move into the reality you want, you need to get clear on the one you are currently in. Observation is where this begins. When you look honestly at your life right now, you can start to identify what is working and worth carrying forward, and what you are ready to leave behind.

The key to this step is neutrality. This is not an exercise in shame or regret. Think of yourself as stepping slightly outside your own life and taking a clear-eyed inventory, the way you would declutter a room. There is a keep pile and there is a release pile, and you approach both without drama.

Remember: Emotional charge anchors you to a timeline. Neutrality is what creates the space to change. The more matter-of-fact you can be here, the more freedom you give yourself to move forward.

Two phrases will guide your reflection in this step. For what you are releasing: "I am no longer available for the timeline where I have been..." For what you are keeping and expanding: "I am so grateful that I..." Use these as your anchors as you write.

If you feel stuck on the gratitude side, get specific and small. Clean water. A roof. A vehicle. Someone who loves you. Gratitude does not need to be grand to be real, and even the smallest acknowledgment of what is already good creates the energetic foundation for more.

Before You Write
Take three slow breaths. Feel the weight of your body in your seat and your feet making contact with the floor. Let your shoulders drop. When you feel settled and present, move into the reflection below.
